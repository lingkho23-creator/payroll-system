Tahap 1: Kewajiban Perusahaan & Profit Bersih

1. Pajak & Asuransi Perusahaan

Dihitung dari TotalRevenue:

Insurance=Revenue×0.03
Bracket RevenuePajak
$0 - $500k15%
$500k - $1M12%
$1M - $2M10%
> $2M7%

2. Kalkulasi Profit Bersih (NetProfit)

Total pengeluaran mencakup belanja bahan baku dan kewajiban:

Expense=Sourcing+Insurance+Tax
NetProfit=Revenue-Expense

Tahap 2 & 3: Pool Utama Gaji (PUG) Staf

Pembagian Pool (PUG)

PUG = NetProfit × Wage% 40% / 50% / 60%

Gaji Pokok (15%)=0.15×PUGTotalStaff Komisi Waktu (15%)=0.15×PUG×StaffHoursTotalHours

Sistem Poin Hustle (70%)

Poin: Receipt (3), Serve (2), Item (1)

HustlePay=(0.7×PUG)×StaffPointsTotalPoints
GrossSalary=Pokok+Waktu+Hustle

Tahap 4: Company Pool & Bonus Manajemen

1. Company Pool

Sisa dana setelah PUG dipisahkan:

CompanyPool=NetProfit-PUG

Alokasi Dana Bonus:

  • Boss Fund: 18% dari Company Pool
  • Supervisor Fund: 12% dari Company Pool
  • Company Vault: Sisanya (Simpanan Brankas)

2. Syarat Kelayakan (Eligible)

Boss: Min. 5 Jam On-Duty

SalaryBoss=BossFundTotalBossEligible

Supervisor: Min. 8 Jam On-Duty

SalarySPV=SPVFundTotalSPVEligible+GrossStaff
